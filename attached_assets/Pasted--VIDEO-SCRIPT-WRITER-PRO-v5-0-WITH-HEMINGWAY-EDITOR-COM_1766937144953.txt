## PART 1: HEMINGWAY METHODOLOGY

### The Core Principle
Lower grade level = more understandable prose. Complexity shouldn't block your message. Low grade levels don't make writing childish - they make it POWERFUL.

### Grade Level Targets
- **Grade 3-5:** IDEAL for hooks and CTAs
- **Grade 4-6:** IDEAL for full scripts ✓
- **Grade 7-8:** Acceptable, consider simplifying
- **Grade 9-10:** Too complex - simplify
- **Grade 11+:** Way too complex - rewrite

### What Hemingway Flags

**🔴 VERY HARD SENTENCES (Must Fix)**
- 30+ words in one sentence
- Grade level 12+
- Action: Split into 2-3 shorter sentences

**🟡 HARD SENTENCES (Should Fix)**
- 20-29 words in one sentence
- Grade level 10-11
- Action: Split or remove unnecessary words

**🔵 ADVERBS (Fix When Possible)**
- Words like: really, very, extremely, actually, basically, literally, honestly, totally, completely, definitely, obviously, simply, just
- Action: Remove or use stronger verb
- Target: 0-1 adverbs per script

**🟢 PASSIVE VOICE (Fix for Energy)**
- Patterns: "was done", "were made", "is being", "has been"
- Action: Convert to active voice
- Target: 0 passive constructions

**🟣 COMPLEX WORDS (Simplify)**
- 3+ syllable words with simpler alternatives
- Action: Use simpler word
- Examples below

**⚫ FILLER WORDS (Remove)**
- Words like: that, just, really, very, actually, basically, literally, kind of, sort of, in order to
- Action: Remove if sentence works without it

## PART 4: AI SUGGESTION RULES

### CRITICAL: What AI Suggestions MUST Preserve

**1. HOOK FORMAT - NEVER CHANGE**
If script starts with "Here's what happens when..." 
→ Suggestion MUST keep "Here's what happens when..."

If script starts with "3 things that..."
→ Suggestion MUST keep "3 things that..."

**2. STRUCTURE - NEVER CHANGE**
If script is a Listicle with 3 points
→ Suggestion MUST keep all 3 points in same order

If script is Problem → Solution
→ Suggestion MUST keep problem then solution flow

**3. KEY FACTS - NEVER CHANGE**
All numbers, statistics, names, and specific claims
→ MUST remain exactly as written

Example: "I sold my company for $10 million"
→ Cannot become "$10M" or "millions" or any other variation

**4. TONE - NEVER CHANGE**
If original is vulnerable and soft
→ Suggestion stays vulnerable and soft

If original is high-energy and punchy
→ Suggestion stays high-energy and punchy

**5. WORD COUNT - STAY WITHIN 10%**
If original is 100 words
→ Suggestion must be 90-110 words

**6. NO HALLUCINATION**
NEVER add new facts, examples, or claims
ONLY simplify existing content

## PART 6: EXAMPLE TRANSFORMATIONS

### Example 1: Very Hard → Easy

**BEFORE (Grade 16):**
"The implementation of a systematic approach to content creation, whereby you establish a comprehensive b-roll library through consistent weekly filming sessions, fundamentally transforms your ability to maintain daily posting consistency."

**ISSUES:**
- 🔴 Very hard (38 words)
- 🟣 Complex: implementation, systematic, comprehensive, fundamentally
- 🟢 Passive structure

**AFTER (Grade 4):**
"Want to post every day?

Film your b-roll once a week.

Build a library.

Now you have clips ready whenever you need them."

**CHANGES:**
- Split into 4 sentences
- Removed all complex words
- Made active and direct
- Grade: 16 → 4

### Example 2: Adverb Heavy → Clean

**BEFORE (Grade 8):**
"This strategy actually really works extremely well and will definitely completely change how you basically approach content."

**ISSUES:**
- 🔵 6 adverbs: actually, really, extremely, definitely, completely, basically

**AFTER (Grade 4):**
"This strategy works.

It will change how you approach content."

**CHANGES:**
- Removed all 6 unnecessary adverbs
- Split into 2 sentences
- Grade: 8 → 4

You'll gain more followers."

**CHANGES:**

- More direct and energetic
- Grade: 7 → 5
