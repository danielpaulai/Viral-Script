## SHOT 11: SEATED POWER POSE

### Gemini Prompt - Copy & Paste:

```
Using the attached reference photo, create a photorealistic, cinematic 9:16 vertical image (1080 x 1920 pixels).

SHOT TYPE: Seated Power Pose

CAMERA POSITION:
- Camera at eye level or slightly below
- Subject seated, camera captures from knees up
- Slight angle (not straight on) for dynamic feel

SUBJECT REQUIREMENTS:
- The person from reference photo (South Asian male, short black hair with fade)
- Seated in a high-end executive chair or designer armchair
- Leaning back confidently, one ankle crossed over knee OR leaning forward with elbows on knees
- Hands: Fingertips together (thinking pose) OR one hand on armrest
- Expression: Confident, contemplative, "I've figured it out" look
- Wearing: Black crew neck shirt, dark tailored pants visible
- Body language exudes success and calm confidence

CINEMATIC REQUIREMENTS:
- Depth of field: f/2.8 (subject sharp, background softly blurred)
- Color grade: Moody, rich, executive aesthetic - deep shadows, warm highlights
- Film grain: Subtle
- Contrast: High, dramatic
- Lens: 50mm equivalent, flattering perspective

BACKGROUND:
- Luxury corner office or high-end study
- Visible: Floor-to-ceiling windows with city skyline view (evening/blue hour), bookshelves with leather-bound books, subtle art on wall, desk lamp casting warm pool of light, whiskey decanter on side table (optional)
- Dark wood and leather textures
- Moody, sophisticated atmosphere
- City lights creating bokeh through windows

LIGHTING:
- Key light: Warm practical lamp from side creating dramatic shadows
- Fill: City ambient glow from windows
- Rim light: Cool blue from window/city behind
- Creates contrast between warm interior and cool exterior
- Rembrandt lighting style on face

TEXT OVERLAY - MUST INCLUDE:
- Text: "SEATED POWER POSE"
- Position: Upper portion of frame (180-300px from top)
- Style: Bold, uppercase, letter-spaced, white text with subtle shadow
- Font: Rethink Sans Bold
- Size: Large, authoritative

MOOD: Executive, contemplative, successful, "CEO energy"
OUTPUT: Photorealistic, looks like Forbes or Fortune magazine cover
```

```
- High contrast, cinematic
- Subject pops from background

- Text: "WORM'S EYE"
- Position: Lower portion of frame, near camera position (1400-1550px from top)
- Style: Bold, uppercase, letter-spaced, white text with strong shadow
```

## SHOT 14: REFLECTION SHOT

### Gemini Prompt - Copy & Paste:

```
Using the attached reference photo, create a photorealistic, cinematic 9:16 vertical image (1080 x 1920 pixels).

SHOT TYPE: Reflection Shot

CAMERA POSITION:
- Camera captures subject AND their reflection simultaneously
- Could be mirror, window, water, or polished surface
- Creates visual depth and artistic composition
- Medium or medium close-up framing

SUBJECT REQUIREMENTS:
- The person from reference photo (South Asian male, short black hair with fade)
- Subject looking at their reflection OR looking at camera while reflection shows profile
- Expression: Thoughtful, introspective, or confident meeting own gaze
- Wearing: Black crew neck shirt
- Creates "two versions" effect - adds depth to composition
- Real subject and reflection should both be partially visible

COMPOSITION OPTIONS (choose one):
- Option A: Subject in foreground (soft focus), reflection in mirror sharp and centered
- Option B: Side profile real, front face in reflection
- Option C: Subject looking out rain-covered window, reflection visible in glass

CINEMATIC REQUIREMENTS:
- Depth of field: f/2-2.8 (play with focus between real and reflection)
- Color grade: Moody, introspective, slightly cool tones with warm accents
- Film grain: Subtle
- Reflections should be realistic with slight imperfections
- Artistic, editorial quality

BACKGROUND:
- High-end bathroom with large mirror and dramatic lighting OR
- Modern space with floor-to-ceiling windows at night (city reflection) OR
- Rainy window with water droplets
- Clean, minimal environment that doesn't distract
- Dark/moody tones

LIGHTING:
- Soft, directional lighting creating dimension
- Light source visible in reflection (window, vanity lights)
- Mood lighting - not flat
- Creates atmosphere and depth
- Reflection may have slightly different lighting quality

TEXT OVERLAY - MUST INCLUDE:
- Text: "REFLECTION SHOT"
- Position: Upper portion of frame (180-320px from top)
- Style: Bold, uppercase, letter-spaced, white text with subtle glow
- Font: Rethink Sans Bold
- Size: Large, artistic

MOOD: Introspective, artistic, thoughtful, "the real me"
OUTPUT: Photorealistic, looks like high-end editorial or film still
```

## SHOT 15: THROUGH FRAME / FOREGROUND ELEMENT

### Gemini Prompt - Copy & Paste:

```
Using the attached reference photo, create a photorealistic, cinematic 9:16 vertical image (1080 x 1920 pixels).

SHOT TYPE: Through Frame / Foreground Element

CAMERA POSITION:
- Camera shoots THROUGH something in the foreground
- Creates natural frame-within-frame
- Adds depth and visual interest
- Subject in middle ground, sharp focus

SUBJECT REQUIREMENTS:
- The person from reference photo (South Asian male, short black hair with fade)
- Positioned in the "window" created by foreground elements
- Close-up or medium close-up framing on subject
- Expression: Engaging, direct, warm smile or thoughtful look
- Wearing: Black crew neck shirt
- Subject is the clear focal point despite foreground elements

FOREGROUND ELEMENT OPTIONS:
- Option A: Shot through doorframe or archway (architectural)
- Option B: Shot through plant leaves (organic, natural)
- Option C: Shot through glass with slight reflections/distortion
- Option D: Shot through ring light or circular object
- Option E: Hands of subject creating frame (fingers forming rectangle)

CINEMATIC REQUIREMENTS:
- Depth of field: f/1.8-2.8 (foreground soft, subject sharp, background soft)
- Three distinct depth layers: foreground (soft), subject (sharp), background (soft)
- Color grade: Rich, cinematic, warm tones
- Film grain: Subtle
- Foreground should be very soft/blurry, almost abstract shapes

BACKGROUND:
- Depends on foreground choice:
- For doorframe: Moody office or studio space
- For plants: Bright, airy indoor space with natural light
- For glass: Urban environment, city lights
- Should complement but not compete with subject
- Soft bokeh, adding depth without distraction

LIGHTING:
- Key light on subject, falling off toward edges
- Foreground elements may be silhouetted or soft
- Creates sense of depth and dimension
- Natural, motivated lighting
- Subject "glows" compared to darker foreground

TEXT OVERLAY - MUST INCLUDE:
- Text: "THROUGH FRAME"
- Position: Upper portion of frame (200-340px from top)
- Style: Bold, uppercase, letter-spaced, white text with shadow
- Font: Rethink Sans Bold
- Size: Large, clean

MOOD: Cinematic, artistic, layered, professional
OUTPUT: Photorealistic, looks like cinematographer's portfolio shot
```
