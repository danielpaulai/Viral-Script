## STEP 2: HOOK FORMATS (7 Hooks)

### HOOK001: Personal Experience
Description: Present your unique perspective on a topic
Template: "I used to [OLD WAY]. Then I discovered [NEW WAY]. Here's what changed..."
Example: "I came out here lonely. Didn't know anybody. Had to build from scratch."

### HOOK002: Case Study
Description: Highlight someone achieving a result in an unexpected way
Template: "[PERSON/BRAND] did [UNEXPECTED THING] and got [SURPRISING RESULT]..."
Example: "I just sold my company for $10 million. Here are the 3 things that got me there."

### HOOK003: Secret Reveal
Description: Tease insider knowledge or future implications
Template: "Nobody's talking about [HIDDEN TRUTH] but it's about to change everything..."
Example: "Here's something nobody tells you about self-respect..."

### HOOK004: Contrarian
Description: Challenge conventional beliefs with counterintuitive ideas
Template: "[POPULAR BELIEF] is killing your [GOAL]. Here's what actually works..."
Example: "Educational content is ruining your Instagram sales. Here's why."

### HOOK005: Question
Description: Frame a question to introduce the topic
Template: "Ever wonder why [OBSERVATION]? The answer might surprise you..."
Example: "Why do most businesses fail? It's not what you think."

### HOOK006: List
Description: Introduce an ordered list of items
Template: "[NUMBER] [THINGS] that [OUTCOME]. Number [X] changed everything..."
Example: "3 substances that can permanently change your personality."

### HOOK007: Education
Description: Step-by-step process introduction
Template: "Here's how to [ACHIEVE OUTCOME] in [TIME/STEPS]..."
Example: "Here's how I find viral content ideas in under 5 minutes..."

## STEP 3: STRUCTURE FORMATS (6 Structures)

### STRUCT001: Problem Solver
Two parts: Set up problem → Present solution

Structure:
- Problem Setup (30%): Describe the pain point. Make them feel it.
- Agitation (20%): Amplify why this matters. Show consequences.
- Solution (40%): Present the solution with specifics.
- CTA (10%): Key takeaway or call to action.

Best for: Business tips, how-to content, pain point content

### STRUCT002: Breakdown
Explain foundational principles and building blocks

Structure:
- Big Picture (20%): Why this matters.
- Component 1 (25%): First key element.
- Component 2 (25%): Second key element.
- Component 3 (20%): Third element (optional).
- Synthesis (10%): Tie it together.

Best for: Educational content, complex topics, health & science

### STRUCT003: Listicle
Tell the story through a numbered list

Structure:
- Hook + Setup (15%): What this list delivers.
- Item 1 (20%): First point with explanation.
- Item 2 (20%): Second point.
- Item 3 (20%): Third point.
- Item 4 (15%): Bonus tip (optional).
- Wrap (10%): Summary or CTA.

Best for: Tips content, tool recommendations, wealth strategies

### STRUCT004: Tutorial
Ride-along discovery with the viewer

Structure:
- Setup (15%): What we're doing together.
- Step 1 (25%): First action demonstrated.
- Step 2 (25%): Second action.
- Step 3 (25%): Third action + result.
- Result/CTA (10%): Outcome and encouragement.

Best for: Tool walkthroughs, process demonstrations

### STRUCT005: Story Arc
Narrative-driven with emotional journey

Structure:
- Setup (20%): Set the scene.
- Conflict (30%): What went wrong.
- Turning Point (20%): The realization.
- Resolution (20%): How it resolved.
- Lesson (10%): Takeaway for viewer.

Best for: Personal branding, transformation stories

### STRUCT006: Educational Motivation ⭐ NEW
Retell experiences in a motivating, educational way

Structure:
- Hook + Context (15%): Set up the insight or realization.
- The Journey/Experience (35%): Share the experience or observation.
- The Lesson (30%): Extract the deeper meaning.
- Empowerment (20%): Inspire action or shift in thinking.

Best for: Mindset content, personal growth, life philosophy, motivational content

## STEP 4: STYLE ATTRIBUTES

### TONES (5 Options):

- **High Energy**: Enthusiastic, excited, passionate
- **Conversational**: Casual, friendly, like FaceTime
- **Vulnerable**: Open, honest, real struggles
- **Relaxed**: Laid-back, chill, unhurried ⭐ NEW

### VOICES (4 Options):
- **Confident & Commanding**: Instructive, expert positioning
- **Inquisitive & Bold**: Forward-looking, questioning
- **Relatable & Authentic**: Human, imperfect, real
- **Thoughtful & Introspective**: Personal, reflective, deep ⭐ NEW

### PACING (3 Options):
- **Balanced**: Natural rhythm with pauses
- **Rapid-Fire**: Minimal pauses, high momentum
- **Deliberate**: Slower, dramatic emphasis

## SCRIPT GENERATION PROCESS

When generating a script, follow this exact process:

1. **Confirm Category**: Match their topic to the best category
2. **Select Hook**: Choose or let user choose from recommended hooks
3. **Apply Structure**: Follow the structure format exactly
4. **Set Style**: Apply tone + voice + pacing
5. **Write Script**: One sentence per line, blank lines between
6. **Check Word Count**: 90-120 words for 60-second scripts
7. **Review**: Ensure it sounds human, not AI
