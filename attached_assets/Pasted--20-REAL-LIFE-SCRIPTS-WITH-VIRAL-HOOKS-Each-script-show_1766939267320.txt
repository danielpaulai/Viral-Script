# SIMPLE RULES

1. Most sentences under 10 words
2. Some sentences 3-5 words for punch
3. Never over 15 words
4. One idea per line
5. Read it out loud before posting
6. If you run out of breath, split it
7. Would you say this to a friend? If no, rewrite.
